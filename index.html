COMPUTER SCIENCE

Computer Science is more than just programming. It is the foundation of modern technology powering smartphones, artificial intelligence, space exploration, healthcare, and even entertainment.


A programming language is a formal way of giving instructions to a computer. It allows humans to communicate with machines and tell them what to do. Instead of using ones and zeros (binary), programming languages provide a structured way to write code that is easier for humans to understand.


Computer science is the study of computer hardware and software. It includes a wide range of interrelated subfields, from machine learning (ML) and artificial intelligence (AI) to cybersecurity and software development. Computer science careers can be found across various industries, including software publishing companies, game development studios, financial services, and even the government.

About Computer Science

Key Areas of Study

  • Algorithms & Data Structures: Efficient problem solving and data organization.
  • Software Engineering: Designing, testing, and maintaining software systems.
  • Artificial Intelligence: Building smart machines and intelligent applications.
  • Cybersecurity: Protecting digital systems from threats.
  • Human-Computer Interaction: Creating user-friendly and accessible technology.
  • Networks & Databases: Managing information and enabling global communication.
